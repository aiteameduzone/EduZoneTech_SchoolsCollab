Program Format

Two flexible delivery options

Both formats end with a practical final project — decided together after sessions 1 or 2, based on students' actual level and interests.

Most Popular
20
sessions across the full academic year

Full-Year Track

Weekly sessions replacing traditional ICT. Depth and pacing ideal for full skill mastery and a major capstone project.

  • ~1 hour per session, weekly
  • Progressive skill building
  • End-of-year capstone showcase
  • Full integration with school calendar
12
sessions distributed over 2 terms

Two-Term Track

A focused, condensed format for schools preferring semester-style delivery. Same hands-on quality, tighter pacing.

  • ~1 hour per session, weekly
  • Terms 1 & 2 (or Terms 2 & 3)
  • Focused mid-year final project
  • Lower commitment trial option
💡
Final project decided after the first 1–2 sessions
This lets us calibrate the capstone to students' actual level, interests, and pace — not a generic template. Every school's capstone is unique.
